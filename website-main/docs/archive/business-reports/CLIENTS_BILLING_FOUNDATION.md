# Clients Billing Foundation

## Clients V1

The `clients` table stores client identity, company, website, region, status, tier, monthly value, currency, owner, follow-up date, notes, and billing fields.

## Billing Fields

- `recurring_interval`
- `next_invoice_at`
- `billing_notes`
- `auto_generate`

## UI

The Clients page now shows active clients, prospects, monthly value, owner, tier, status, next billing date, quick add, and manual billing actions.

## Roadmap

1. Link clients to finance records and receipts.
2. Generate invoice records from client billing settings.
3. Add reminders for overdue and upcoming invoices.
4. Add client portal or internal client detail pages.
5. Add employee ownership and HR-linked assignment later.
