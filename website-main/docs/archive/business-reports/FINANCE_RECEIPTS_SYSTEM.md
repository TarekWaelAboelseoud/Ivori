# Finance And Receipts System

## Finance V1

`finance_records` tracks invoices, payments, expenses, refunds, subscriptions, tax, payroll, and other records. The Finance page shows expected revenue, collected revenue, pending invoices, overdue value, expenses, and net cash.

## Receipts V1

`receipts` tracks vendor, category, amount, currency, purchase date, payment method, client/project links, file URL, status, and notes. The Receipts page supports manual entry, search, review status, monthly totals, and export-ready table structure.

## Automation Foundation

Finance records include recurring interval, next invoice date, billing notes, and `auto_generate`. This supports future invoice generation, payment reminders, and receipt creation after payment.
